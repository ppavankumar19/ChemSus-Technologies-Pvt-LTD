About ChemSus Technologies

A young Indian specialty and sustainable chemicals company focused on high-quality, value-added chemical solutions.

ChemSus Technologies Private Limited is classified as an Indian non-government company limited by shares and is a young Indian specialty and sustainable chemicals company focused on developing and manufacturing high-quality, value-added chemical products for diverse industries.

Who We Are

ChemSus Technologies Private Limited is a private, unlisted Indian company incorporated on 29 May 2023 and registered with RoC–Shillong in Assam, India. The company operates from North Guwahati, Kamrup, building from a strong regional base while serving customers across India and beyond.

What We Do

ChemSus Technologies operates in the broader specialty chemicals and allied sectors, spanning pharmaceuticals, performance ingredients, and advanced intermediates.

Our scope includes the development and manufacture of customized specialty chemicals, functional additives, and high-purity intermediates tailored to customer and industry needs, including medicinal applications, nutraceuticals, agrochemicals, and food & cosmetic additives.

Industries We Serve

  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Biochemicals
  • Green Chemistry
  • Personal Care

  • Agrochemicals

Our Strengths

  • High-purity chemicals (up to 99%)
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable processes
  • Trusted academic roots (IIT Guwahati)
  • Reliable delivery and strong technical support
  • Scalable production for diverse customer needs

Purpose & Vision

ChemSus Technologies positions itself at the intersection of chemistry and sustainability, aligning its portfolio with green and resource-efficient chemical innovation.

Our vision is to enable safer, more efficient, and environmentally responsible specialty chemical solutions that support better health, cleaner processes, and more sustainable value chains for partner industries.

Innovation & Sustainability Focus

The company leverages incubation and technology support ecosystems to design specialty chemistry routes that minimize waste, energy use, and environmental footprint.

This innovation-driven approach enables ChemSus Technologies to develop next-generation specialty products, from bio-based intermediates to cleaner process aids, guided by the principles of green chemistry and circularity.
